About us

Global Midstream Services (GMS) was founded with a primary focus on pipeline integrity, recommendations on inspection methodologies, engineering technical support, and repair methods and products such as composite sleeves.

These enhancements aim to improve operational safety and ensure adherence to regulatory compliance.

Our Philosophy

At GMS, our philosophy is built on three principles:

  • Safety First: Every decision, project, and operation is executed with the highest safety standards.
  • Technical Excellence: We provide accurate, reliable, and data-driven solutions.
  • Client Partnership: We work closely with operators to understand their assets, challenges, and long-term goals.

Mision

Our mission is to deliver high-quality pipeline integrity, and engineering support services that enhance the reliability, safety, and performance to the midstream sector.

Vision

To be a trusted leader in midstream integrity sector and be recognized for our technical expertise, innovation, and commitment to operational excellence.
