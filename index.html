GLOBAL MIDSTREAM SERVICES

GMS assists pipeline operators with integrity management by offering the following services:

  • Pipeline Integrity Management
  • Project Management
  • Engineering and Technical Support
  • Pigging system design
  • Consulting services
  • AGM surveys & pig tracking

Our services

Project management

Assist clients with pre-planning assessments, execution, and pipeline rehabilitation initiatives.

Pipeline Integrity Management

Support the development, implementation and enhancement of standards and procedures for different types of assessments and repair methods.

Engineering & Technical Support

Assess and improve field anomaly measurement techniques to increase inspection effectiveness and efficiency.

Pigging System Design

Assist in completing pipeline questionnaires and reviewing information to determine pipeline suitability for in-line inspection.

Consulting Services

Offer recommendations for assessment and mitigation strategies related to pipelines and facilities.

Welding Services

GMS provides (third-party) comprehesive welding solutions for pipeline make-ready projects and testing requirements.

Our products

Composite Sleeves

Supply industry-approved composite sleeves for both temporary and permanent repair applications.

Chemical products

GMS offers and recommends specialize chemical (third-party) for pipeline cleaning, suitable for batch processes or continuous injection.
