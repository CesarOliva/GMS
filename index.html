GLOBAL MIDSTREAM SERVICES

GMS assists pipeline operators with integrity management, NDE inspection, technical engineering, consulting, pigging system design, AGM surveys, and pig tracking. GMS commits to safe, reliable midstream solutions emphasizing safety and operational excellence.

Our services

We offer comprehensive services in pipeline integrity, nondestructive examination (NDE) inspection, and engineering support, specifically designed for midstream and energy sector clients.

Project Managment

Provide clients with assistence in pre-planing assessmenrs and pipeline rehabilitation initiatives.

Pipeline Integrity Management

Support the development, implementation, and enhancement of standards, procedures, and best practices for In-Line Inspection.

Engineering & Technical Support

Assess and improve field anomaly measurement techniques to increase inspection effectiveness and efficiency.

Pigging System Design

Assist in completing pipeline questionnaires and reviewing information to determine pipeline suitability for in-line inspection.

Consulting Services

Offer recommendations for assessment and mitigation strategies related to pipelines and facilities. GMS also provides representation in Latin America.

Non-Destructive Examination

Execute NDE services to identify defects or irregularities in materials, components, and structures.
