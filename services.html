Our Services

We offer engineering, procurement and construction management (EPCM) services for oil and gas facilities, refineries, alternative fuels, petrochemical plants, residential and tourism projects both nationally and internationally.

Feasibility Study

GMS provides conceptual engineering services to address key project questions before work begins. We collaborate closely with clients to understand requirements and objectives, delivering optimal process solutions tailored to your needs.

  • • Requirements compilation
  • • Technology alternatives evaluation
  • • Preliminary process design
  • • Process simulations & mass balances
  • • Cost estimation
  • • 3D models & material lists

3D Laser Scanning

We perform laser scanning of any building, plant, equipment, or structure.
The fastest way to obtain a digital representation, converting physical spaces into precise point clouds. It captures an accurate, detailed digital model.

  • • Accuracy up to 2 millimeters
  • • Rapid capture of exact measurements and elevations
  • • Quick generation of drawings, sections, and cuts
  • • 3D imaging of complex geometries and environments in minutes

Basic & Detailed Engineering

GMS develops plans, technical specifications, and bidding documents that capture client requirements, basic specs, schedules, and budget scope. These initial plans are used for project evaluation and quotation, not construction.

  • • Plans & Sketches
  • • Calculation Reports
  • • Techincal Specifications

3D Modeling

3D modeling involves creating three-dimensional objects using CAD software. At ODIN, we develop high-value plant, infrastructure, and building designs using both conceptual and detailed design tools.

  • • Intelligent 3D model development
  • • Detailed drawing extraction
  • • Material lists for fabrication
  • • Cloud-based BIM collaboration

Engineering Analysis

GMS optimizes projects using integrated analysis, visualization, and simulation tools to ensure design performance and reliability.

Finite Element Analysis (FEA)

We predict design performance through linear, non-linear, thermal, and dynamic analysis.

Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)

Our simulations intelligently predict how liquids and gases will behave in your systems.

Composite Sleeves

We supply industry-approved composite sleeves designed for both temporary and permanent pipeline repairs. Our advanced composite technology provides reliable, long-lasting solutions for maintaining pipeline integrity.

Temporary Repairs

Quick-deployment sleeves for emergency situations and temporary containment.

Permanent Solutions

Durable, long-term repair sleeves that restore full pipeline integrity.

Welding Services

We deliver comprehensive welding solutions for make-ready projects and pipeline testing requirements. Our certified welders and advanced techniques ensure quality, durability, and compliance with industry standards.

Make-Ready Projects

Complete welding solutions for project preparation and construction phases.

Pipeline Testing

Specialized welding for pressure testing and integrity verification.

Chemical Products

We provide specialized chemical solutions for comprehensive pipeline cleaning and maintenance. Our products are engineered for optimal performance in both batch processes and continuous injection systems.

  • • Advanced cleaning agents for various pipeline contaminants
  • • Corrosion inhibitors and scale prevention formulas
  • • Biocide treatments for microbial control
  • • Environmentally conscious formulations
  • • Customized solutions for specific pipeline conditions
