Our services

At GMS, we specialize in managing pipeline integrity projects and delivering focused engineering support designed specifically for pipeline operators. Our comprehensive services ensure that your pipeline systems are not only efficient but also safe and reliable, allowing you to operate with confidence in every situation.

Project Management

We provide comprehensive project management support for pipeline projects, from initial assessment through successful execution and rehabilitation. Our experienced teams ensure your projects are delivered on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Pipeline Integrity Management

We support the development, implementation, and enhancement of standards and procedures for various pipeline integrity assessments and repair methods, ensuring operational safety, reliability, and regulatory compliance.

Engineering & Technical Support

We provide expert engineering and technical support to assess and enhance field anomaly measurement techniques, significantly increasing the effectiveness and efficiency of your inspection programs.

Pigging System Design

We assist in completing detailed pipeline questionnaires and reviewing critical information to accurately determine pipeline suitability for in-line inspection, ensuring optimal pigging system design and operational success.

Consulting Services

We provide expert consulting services, offering strategic recommendations for assessment and mitigation to enhance the safety, integrity, and performance of your pipelines and facilities.

Welding Services

We deliver comprehensive welding solutions for make-ready projects and pipeline testing requirements. Our certified welders and advanced techniques ensure quality, durability, and compliance with industry standards.

Project Execution

Complete oversight and management of pipeline integrity projects from inception to completion.

Assessments & Repair Methods

Our team provides appropriate assessment and repair methods using technical engineering calculations derived from operators' pipeline technical information.

AGM / Pig Tracking

Composite Sleeves

GMS offers industry-approved composite sleeves designed for both temporary and permanent pipeline repairs. Our advanced composite are D.O.T. /C.S.A / ASME PCC-2, ISO TS-24817 compliant, reliable and long-lasting solutions for maintainng pipeline integrity.

Chemical Products

GMS provides and endorses specialized chemicals from third-party suppliers for pipeline cleaning, which are suitable for both batch processes and continous injection applications.
